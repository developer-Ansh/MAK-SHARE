Greetings everyone, MAK SHARE was created to share any files easily and quickly. Any person can share any files at his/her fingertips. The best part about MAK SHARE is you can use this app very easily, it is very easy to use and the UI of this APP is very clean so anybody of any age can use this app without any issues. MAK SHARE was developed by Ansh Solanki at the age of 12. This app was developed when 82 Chinese apps were banned and some of them were very useful, so our developer thought let's create an Indian APP which can share files quickly, and by doing this we have also supported AATMANIRBHAR BHARAT campaign.

MAK SHARE

MAK SHARE IS APP IN WHICH YOU CAN SHARE FILES EASILY AND INSTANTLY. YOU HAVE TO JUST TAP ON SEND OR RECEIVE BUTTON AND CHOOSE THE FILE THAN THE SENDER TAP ON "Set up hotspot" BUTTON AND RECEIVER TAP ON "Scan QR Code" BUTTON THAT'S IT. AND THE FILE WILL BE TRANSFERRED WITH 10 - 15 MBPS SPEED
